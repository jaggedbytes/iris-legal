Iris

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Iris is a phone-first AI companion demo operated by Jagged Bytes. This policy explains what information the Iris demo processes when you use voice calls, the operator or trusted-contact dashboard, and approval-gated SMS.

What we collect

  • Phone numbers used to place Iris calls and to send check-in SMS through Twilio
  • Display names and relationships for enrolled trusted contacts
  • Dashboard authentication credentials or tokens you configure for access control
  • Consented call summaries and user-stated memories when summary retention is enabled
  • Operational records needed to send and audit SMS (for example outbox status), without storing raw call transcripts in those records

What we do not store

  • Call audio recordings
  • Raw phone transcripts as durable database content (transcript text is held in memory only for consented summary extraction after a call, then discarded)
  • Shield scam-assessment input, red-flag labels, risk scores, or scenario details in durable storage or in SMS content

How SMS works

Trusted contacts are enrolled by the Iris account holder or an authorized operator in the Iris dashboard, where the contact’s name and mobile number are provided for care-related check-in messages. Before Iris sends an SMS, the person speaking with Iris must clearly approve the recipient and message on a live phone call (or approve Iris’s fixed Shield check-in alert text).

Recipients may reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

How we use information

  • To place and operate Iris voice calls
  • To send approval-gated Bridge or Shield check-in messages to trusted contacts
  • To show a privacy-preserving dashboard timeline and consented call recap
  • To maintain required operational audit and SMS delivery records

Processors

We use Twilio for voice and SMS delivery and OpenAI for live conversation and ephemeral safety assessment. Those providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

Retention

Consented summaries and memories are retained while the demo account remains active and consent remains granted. SMS outbox and related operational records are retained as needed to operate and troubleshoot message delivery. You may contact us to request deletion of demo account data where feasible.
